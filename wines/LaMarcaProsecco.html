La Marca Prosecco

Overview

La Marca Prosecco is a sparkling wine from Italy's Veneto region, crafted from the Glera grape. Known for its crisp and refreshing style, it has become America's #1 Italian Prosecco, celebrated for its delicate, floral palate. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Tasting Notes

  • Aroma: Fruity aromas of green apple, juicy peach, and ripe lemon, with hints of minerality. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Palate: Crisp acidity with subtle hints of green apple and ripe lemon, leading to a light and refreshing finish. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
