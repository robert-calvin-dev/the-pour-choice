Fat Bastard Syrah

Overview

Fat Bastard Syrah, produced in the Pays d'Oc region of France, is a testament to the collaboration between Thierry and Guy. This wine showcases the rich terroir of the Languedoc-Roussillon area, offering a robust and flavorful experience for wine enthusiasts. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Tasting Notes

  • Aroma: The nose displays a spicy, yet fresh profile containing tinges of olive, clove, and sweet blueberry. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Palate: On the palate, it exhibits a robust body with invigorating flavors of paprika, tobacco, and peony. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Accolades

The 2021 vintage received an 89-point rating from Wine Enthusiast, highlighting its juicy plum and oregano aromas, light palate with ample acidity, and hints of boysenberry, plum, bell pepper, and round tannins. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Food Pairing

  • Pairs excellently with red meats, especially grilled or roasted dishes.
  • Complements lamb and spicy foods.
  • Enjoy with full-bodied cheeses for a harmonious pairing. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Serving Suggestions

For optimal enjoyment, serve at room temperature. While delightful when young, this wine can also develop additional complexity with short-term aging.

Conclusion

Fat Bastard Syrah exemplifies the quality and distinctiveness of French wines from the Pays d'Oc region, offering a perfect balance of spicy aromas, robust flavors, and a lingering finish that has captivated wine lovers worldwide.
