Burrowing Owl Merlot

Overview

Burrowing Owl Merlot is a rich and well-structured BC VQA wine from the South Okanagan, known for its ripe fruit flavors and smooth, lingering finish.

Tasting Notes

  • Aroma: Plum, dark cherry, vanilla, and toasted oak.
  • Palate: Smooth and velvety with layers of black fruit, chocolate, and spice.

Accolades

The 2022 vintage was awarded **92 points** by Wine Align, recognized for its balance and refined tannins.

Food Pairing

  • Pairs perfectly with **roast duck**, mushroom risotto, and **pasta with red sauce**.
  • Complements **charcuterie boards** with soft cheeses.

Serving Suggestions

Best served at **16-18°C (60-65°F)**. Let breathe for **30 minutes** before serving.

Conclusion

Burrowing Owl Merlot is an elegant and approachable wine, showcasing the best of BC VQA craftsmanship.
