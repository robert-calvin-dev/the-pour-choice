Black Hills Nota Bene

Overview

Black Hills Nota Bene is an iconic Bordeaux-style blend from the Okanagan Valley, BC. This highly sought-after BC VQA wine is known for its depth, structure, and aging potential.

Tasting Notes

  • Aroma: Cassis, black cherry, cedar, and dark chocolate.
  • Palate: Rich layers of blackberry, plum, tobacco, and spice with firm tannins and a long finish.

Accolades

The 2021 vintage earned a **94-point** rating from Wine Spectator, lauded for its balance, intensity, and finesse.

Food Pairing

  • Perfect with **ribeye steak**, braised short ribs, and game meats.
  • Pairs well with **truffle risotto** and **hard cheeses** like aged cheddar.

Serving Suggestions

Decant for **1-2 hours** before serving at **16-18°C (60-65°F)** to fully appreciate its complexity.

Conclusion

Black Hills Nota Bene is a flagship Canadian wine, offering a sophisticated and unforgettable tasting experience.
