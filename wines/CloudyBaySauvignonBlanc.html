Cloudy Bay Sauvignon Blanc

Overview

Cloudy Bay Sauvignon Blanc is an iconic wine from New Zealand's Marlborough region, renowned for its vibrant and expressive character. Established in 1985, Cloudy Bay played a pivotal role in bringing New Zealand Sauvignon Blanc to international acclaim.

Tasting Notes

  • Aroma: Enticing aromas of ripe passionfruit, white peach, and a hint of lime.
  • Palate: Concentrated tropical flavors with nectarine and floral notes. A crisp acidity balances the wine, leading to a long, refreshing finish.

Accolades

The 2024 vintage received a 93-point rating from Wine Review Online, highlighting its complexity, depth of flavors, and subtle chalky finish.

Food Pairing

  • Pairs excellently with seafood dishes, particularly smoked salmon.
  • Goes well with grilled chicken, calamari, and mildly spiced vegetarian dishes.

Serving Suggestions

For the best experience, serve chilled between 8-10°C (46-50°F). While delightful when young, this wine can also age gracefully for up to 10 years, developing additional complexity over time.

Conclusion

Cloudy Bay Sauvignon Blanc exemplifies the quality and distinctiveness of New Zealand wines, offering a perfect balance of tropical fruit flavors, refreshing acidity, and a lingering finish that has captivated wine lovers worldwide.
