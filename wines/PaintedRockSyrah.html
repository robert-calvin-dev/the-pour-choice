Painted Rock Syrah

Overview

Painted Rock Syrah is a premium wine from the Okanagan Valley, showcasing the region's rich terroir. This BC VQA wine is known for its bold structure and elegant complexity, consistently earning high praise among wine critics.

Tasting Notes

  • Aroma: Dark berries, cracked black pepper, smoked meat, and floral hints of violet.
  • Palate: Full-bodied with juicy blackberry, dark cherry, and spice, balanced by velvety tannins and a lingering finish.

Accolades

The 2022 vintage received a 93-point rating from Wine Align, commended for its deep concentration, balance, and refined structure.

Food Pairing

  • Perfect with **grilled lamb**, BBQ ribs, or beef tenderloin.
  • Pairs well with **earthy mushroom dishes** and **aged cheeses**.

Serving Suggestions

Best served at **16-18°C (60-65°F)**. Decant for an hour to enhance its aromatic complexity.

Conclusion

Painted Rock Syrah exemplifies the world-class quality of BC VQA wines, offering power, elegance, and a beautifully structured experience.
