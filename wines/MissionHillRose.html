Mission Hill Reserve Rosé

Overview

Mission Hill Reserve Rosé is a distinguished wine from the Okanagan Valley in British Columbia, Canada. Crafted by Mission Hill Family Estate, this rosé is known for its vibrant and refreshing character, embodying the essence of the region's terroir. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Tasting Notes

  • Aroma: Delicate scents of fresh raspberry, red berries, melon, soft citrus zest, and mineral notes. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  • Palate: Crisp and fresh with flavors of watermelon, strawberries, and rhubarb, leading to a dry, medium-bodied finish with medium-plus acidity. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Accolades

The 2022 vintage received a 90-point rating from Gismondi on Wine, highlighting its clean, crisp, and bright profile with vibrant red fruit flavors. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Food Pairing

  • Pairs excellently with summer salads and seafood Alfredo dishes. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Complements light pasta dishes, grilled chicken, and soft cheeses.
  • Enjoy with fresh fruits and mildly spiced dishes.

Serving Suggestions

For optimal enjoyment, serve chilled between 8-10°C (46-50°F). This wine is best enjoyed young to appreciate its fresh and vibrant character.

Conclusion

Mission Hill Reserve Rosé exemplifies the quality and distinctiveness of Okanagan Valley wines, offering a harmonious balance of delicate aromas, crisp acidity, and a refreshing finish that appeals to wine enthusiasts worldwide.
