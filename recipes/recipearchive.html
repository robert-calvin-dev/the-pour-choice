Berry Citrus Sangria

A vibrant and refreshing sangria with mixed berries and citrus.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle red wine (such as Tempranillo, Rioja, or Merlot)
  • 1/4 cup brandy or orange liqueur (Cointreau/Triple Sec)
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 orange, sliced into rounds
  • 1 apple, chopped
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • A few fresh mint leaves (for garnish)
  • Ice cubes
  • 1‚Äì2 cups club soda or sparkling water (for a lighter, fizzy touch)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine red wine, brandy, orange juice,
    and honey/sugar. Stir until fully dissolved.
  2. Add the sliced oranges, chopped apples, pineapple chunks, blackberries,
    raspberries, and blueberries. Stir gently.
  3. Let it sit in the fridge for at least 2 hours (overnight is best for deeper flavors).
  4. Before serving, add ice cubes and club soda for fizz.
  5. Pour into glasses, garnish with mint leaves, and enjoy!

This berry-citrus sangria is perfect for summer gatherings or a cozy evening!

White Wine Sangria

A light and refreshing white wine sangria infused with tropical and citrus flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle white wine (such as Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Grigio, or Riesling)
  • 1/4 cup peach schnapps or orange liqueur (Cointreau/Triple Sec)
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 orange, sliced into rounds
  • 1 lemon, sliced into rounds
  • 1 lime, sliced into rounds
  • 1/2 cup mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup green grapes, halved
  • A few fresh mint leaves (for garnish)
