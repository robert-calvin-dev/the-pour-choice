Jackson Family Wines' Sustainability Initiatives

Jackson Family Wines, led by Katie Jackson, is spearheading ambitious sustainability initiatives to make the company **climate positive by 2050**. Their comprehensive "Rooted for Good" program focuses on **environmental responsibility, regenerative farming, water conservation, and global climate action**.

Commitment to Climate Action

Jackson Family Wines has pledged to **cut greenhouse gas emissions by 50% by 2030** and become **climate positive by 2050**—without relying on carbon offsets. Key initiatives include:

  • Investing in **renewable energy**, including **solar arrays and utility-scale wind turbines**.
  • Transitioning to **zero-emission vehicles** for vineyard and winery operations.
  • Implementing **soil carbon sequestration** in vineyards to absorb CO₂.

Renewable Energy Leadership

Jackson Family Wines is the **U.S. wine industry's leader in renewable energy adoption**. Currently, over **30% of their winemaking operations** are powered by on-site solar energy, with plans to **increase this to 50% by 2025**.

Regenerative Farming Practices

All Jackson Family estate vineyards are transitioning to **regenerative farming by 2030**. This holistic approach focuses on:

  • Improving **soil health** to naturally enhance biodiversity.
  • Promoting **cover cropping and composting** to support sustainable ecosystems.
  • Reducing synthetic fertilizers and pesticides to protect the land.

Water Conservation Efforts

Recognizing water as a vital resource, Jackson Family Wines is implementing innovative water conservation techniques to **reuse water, protect local habitats, and recharge groundwater aquifers**.

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) Initiatives

Beyond environmental efforts, JFW is also committed to **fostering workplace diversity and social equity**. They have:

  • Established **DEI steering committees and councils**.
  • Enhanced recruitment to **attract a diverse talent pool**.
  • Developed programs to **support employees and build stronger communities**.

Global Climate Action & Collaboration

Katie Jackson co-founded **International Wineries for Climate Action (IWCA)**, an organization uniting wineries worldwide to commit to **science-based decarbonization practices**.

Financial Impact of Sustainability

Jackson Family Wines’ sustainability initiatives have also proven financially successful, generating **over $26 million in savings**, with approximately **$4 million in annual returns** on sustainability investments.

Final Thoughts

Jackson Family Wines is setting a new standard for **sustainability in the wine industry**. Their long-term commitment to climate action, renewable energy, regenerative farming, and DEI initiatives makes them a leader in **environmental and social responsibility**.
