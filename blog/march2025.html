Auteur Wines Opens New Tasting Room in Russian River Valley

Auteur Wines, known for its handcrafted Pinot Noir and Chardonnay, has officially opened its **new estate tasting room** in the heart of **Russian River Valley**, offering an unparalleled wine-tasting experience.

Spectacular Setting

The new winery sits on an **8.2-acre estate**, featuring a stunning **50-foot glass wall** that showcases breathtaking vineyard views. Located near the historic Raford Inn, the tasting room is designed to blend modern elegance with the rustic beauty of Sonoma’s landscape.

Unique Wine Tasting Experiences

At this new location, visitors can enjoy **curated, immersive wine tastings** that go beyond traditional flights:

  • Appellation Series: A guided tasting exploring blends from different coastal regions.
  • Single-Vineyard Series: Experience the unique character of each vineyard.
  • “Be Here Now” Blind Tasting: A sensory adventure where wines are served without labels, encouraging guests to rely on taste alone.
  • “Musings of the Clones”: A deep dive into Pinot Noir and Chardonnay clones.
  • Library Tasting: A rare opportunity to taste aged Auteur vintages.

Small-Lot Production, Exceptional Quality

Auteur Wines remains committed to **small-batch, high-quality winemaking**, currently producing just **5,000 cases per year**. However, the new estate winery allows for an expansion of up to **8,500 cases annually**, ensuring continued excellence while meeting demand.

Financing & Investment

The expansion was made possible through a **$7.15 million construction loan**, allowing Auteur to develop a **state-of-the-art gravity-fed winery** while preserving the natural beauty of the estate.

Luxury Meets Sustainability

The new estate embraces **sustainable practices**, using a gravity-flow design that minimizes energy consumption while enhancing the winemaking process. The repurposed **former hops ranch** also reflects a commitment to preserving local agricultural heritage.

Why This Matters

This move positions Auteur Wines as a **destination winery** while maintaining its **boutique exclusivity**. The focus on **experience-driven tastings**, **sustainable winemaking**, and **intimate hospitality** aligns with modern wine industry trends.

Plan Your Visit

Tastings are **by appointment only**, ensuring a personalized and relaxed experience. Don't miss the chance to explore **one of the most exciting new tasting rooms in Sonoma County**.
